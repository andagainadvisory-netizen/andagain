Choose your structure

Free Zone or Mainland, chosen around your strategy

Two paths, different strengths. The right one depends on your market, activity, and growth plans. It is the first thing we help you decide.

Option 01

Free Zone

✓ 100% foreign ownership
✓ Lower setup cost, faster licensing
✓ Tax incentives on qualifying income
✓ Flexible visa quotas
Best for international trade, services, and lean overhead.
Option 02

Mainland

✓ Direct access to the full UAE market
✓ Eligible for government contracts
✓ Operate anywhere in the UAE
✓ Broader activity scope
Best for local market focus, retail, and expansion.
Before choosing, weigh cost, visa needs, banking, tax treatment, licensed activity, and where your customers are. Qualifying Free Zone income can benefit from 0% corporate tax, subject to conditions we assess properly, never assume.
